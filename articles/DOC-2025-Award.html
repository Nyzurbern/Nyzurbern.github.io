DOC 2025 Best Presentation Award

I was awarded the DOC Best Presentation Award for delivering the best presentation and drone solution in the Drone Odyssey Competition (DOC) competition (B2 Category) with my group. This recognition highlights our ability to effectively communicate complex ideas and showcase my an my team's work in a compelling manner.

To win, I had to present my group's project alongside my team to a panel of judges and answer their questions. My team & I were able to effectively communicate our ideas, answer the judges' questions appropriately, and demonstrate the value of our project, which impressed the judges and earned us the award.
