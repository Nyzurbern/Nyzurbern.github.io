This certification validates my understanding of Swift syntax, basic programming principles, and the ability to create simple applications using Swift.

To pass, a score of 700/1000 was required, and I passed with flying colours, attaining a score of 980/1000! I was given the opportunity to take the exam due to a programme I joined known as Swift Accelerator.
