Goalaire: Your Goals, Your Characters, Your Journey

Overview

Goalaire is a unique goal-setting app that allows users to create and customize their own characters, which represent their goals. Users can set specific goals, make progress measurable using sub-goals, and keep their characters alive throughout their journey. The app provides a gamified experience, making goal-setting more engaging and motivating for users of all ages.

What was used

  • Language(s): Swift, SwiftUI
  • Framework(s): SwiftData, State Management, UserNotifications
  • Tools: Xcode, GitHub, Figma
  • Platform: iOS

My Contributions

  • Helped design the app's user interface and user experience.
  • Helped implement the core functionality of the app, including goal creation, character customization, and progress tracking.
  • Created the data saving and persistence logic to ensure user data is safely stored and retrieved.
  • Assisted in testing and debugging to ensure the app's stability and performance using my personal phone.

Features

  • Set big goals with descriptions, your selected character and hard deadlines.
  • Set subgoals (with deadlines) to assist you in achieving your big goals.
  • Use coins obtained from completing subgoals to feed your characters.
  • When a big goal is due, a prompt to reflect on your journey will initiate.
  • Look back on your past reflections in the journal to gain new insights.

What I learned

I learned to work with SwiftData for efficient data management and built the entire system from scratch after having previously never used it. I also learned state management for keeping data up to date across different parts of the app and to make a design that is both functional and aesthetically pleasing while sticking to Apple's best practices. It also gave me experience in developing an app from just a drawing to a functional product in a tight deadline. (Fun fact: The bulk of this app was coded within 1 week and finished in the next week.)
